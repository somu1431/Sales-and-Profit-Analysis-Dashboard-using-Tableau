**Project Objective:**

Develop an **interactive Sales and Profit Analysis Dashboard in Tableau to track key business KPIs** and **analyze performance trends** across **multiple departments.**

**Enable stakeholders** from **Sales, Marketing, Finance, and Leadership teams** to make **data-driven** decisions **through** dynamic visuals and drill-down features.

**Purpose of Analysis:**

To provide a unified platform that visualizes sales performance, profit margins, and regional trends, helping decision-makers identify growth opportunities and improve strategic planning.

**Importance of Analyzing Sales and Profit Data:**

Identify **top-performing products, regions,** and **customer segments.**

**Evaluate profitability** and detect **underperforming areas.**

Support **revenue forecasting** and **business strategy optimization.**

**Role of Reports:**

Track **KPIs** such as Total Sales, Profit Margin, and Regional Performance.

Allow stakeholders to **drill down** by region, category, or time period.

**Enhance data visibility** for Sales, Marketing, and Finance departments.

Provide **actionable insights** for **improving revenue** and **profitability.**

**Technical & Soft Skills:**

**Technical Skills:**

**Connected** and blended data from multiple sources for analysis.

Applied **data joins** and blending techniques for accuracy.

**Created** calculated fields and table calculations for **KPIs.**

Designed **interactive visuals** with filters, parameters, and dashboard actions.

**Used** trend charts, heatmaps, and bar visuals to represent **sales** and **profit distribution.**

**Implemented** dashboard actions (filter, highlight, URL) for interactivity.

Shared dashboards through **Tableau Public**/Server for easy access.

**Soft Skills:**

**Strong analytical** and visualization mindset.

Translating **complex metrics** into simple, interactive visuals.

User-focused dashboard design and **attention to detail.**

**Effective communication** and **presentation** of business insights.

**Key Insights & Outcomes:**

Delivered a **single interactive Tableau dashboard** accessible to all key departments.

**Provided drillable insights** into product, regional, and **profitability trends.**
Enabled leadership teams to monitor sales and profit **KPIs** in real time.

Supported **data-driven** strategy and improved decision-making efficiency.
